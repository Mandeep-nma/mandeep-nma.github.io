About

CA. Analyst. Your Numbers Person.

I'm Mandeep Singh — a Chartered Accountant and ACCA Finalist with over three years of financial reporting experience at HSBC. Today, I work as an independent finance consultant helping firms in the service sector and CPA firms across Canada and the US achieve clean books and actionable financial visibility.

My edge isn't just technical — it's the ability to connect your numbers to decisions. Whether it's cleaning up years of messy books, uncovering revenue leakage, or building a monthly reporting rhythm, I work like a controller, not a bookkeeper.
